Selected Publications

A Joint Model of Intent Determination and Slot Filling for Spoken Language Understanding

Two major tasks in spoken language understanding (SLU) are intent determination (ID) and slot filling (SF). Based on the idea that the intent and semantic slots of a sentence are correlative, we propose a joint model for both tasks. Gated recurrent unit (GRU) is used to learn the representation of each time step, by which the label of each slot is predicted. Meanwhile, a max-pooling layer is employed to capture global features of a sentence for intent classification. The representations are shared by two tasks and the model is trained by a united loss function.
Xiaodong Zhang, Houfeng Wang
In IJCAI 2016
